About Me

My name is Stephanie. I'm 32 and I love surfing, photography and spending time with my family. My partner, Jess, and I own and operate our own sand footwear company, ShocSox, in which we design and manufacture protective footwear for beach athletes and enthusiasts. We spend most of our spare time at the beach or outdoors doing other various activites in nature such as hiking, mountainbiking, skateboarding, rollerblading or just going for a walk. We have two kids, four rats and a dog, but I hope to add another dog to our family soon. A random fact about me is that I am 6 feet tall. In the future, I hope to graduate from CSUMB and open my own design firm in Maui.
